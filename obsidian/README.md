# Guillermo Obsidian Vault (Operational)

This vault is used by Guillermo for homelab implementation tracking.

## Structure
- 00-Inbox/ — quick captures
- 01-Projects/ — phased rollout plans and active projects
- 02-Runbooks/ — repeatable operational procedures
- 03-Decisions/ — ADR-style decision logs
- 04-Risks/ — risk register and mitigations
- 05-Incidents/ — incident timelines and RCAs
- 06-Reports/ — daily/weekly reports
- 07-Reference/ — architecture and static references
- Templates/ — reusable note templates

## Conventions
- Prefer one topic per note.
- Use wikilinks heavily (`[[...]]`) for cross-linking.
- Keep status fields at top of project/runbook notes.
- Add ISO timestamps in UTC in operational notes.
